People didn't claim it because they didn't know it existed.

Some is forecasting variance. But hardship grants and housing support are demand-driven — the money was there because Parliament expected people to need it.

What this tool does

It reads the law so you don't have to. 54 questions derive ~107 facts about your situation. Those facts are tested against 75 machine-readable benefit rules. In under 5 minutes, you get a list of everything the law says you may be entitled to — with legal citations, dollar amounts, and what to say at your appointment.

All evaluation happens in your browser. Nothing is stored. Your privacy is protected, we don't need to know, we just need you to know.
